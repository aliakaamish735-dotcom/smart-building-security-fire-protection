The main parts used in the circuit

# Components List

## Main Controller

- 1 × Arduino Uno microcontroller board

## Entry Security System

- 1 × TCRT5000 infrared optical sensor
- 1 × HX711 load-cell amplifier
- 1 × Load cell
- 1 × 4×4 matrix keypad
- 1 × PCF8574 I²C expander
- 1 × SG90 servo motor

## Fire Protection System

- 1 × DHT11 temperature and humidity sensor
- 1 × YF-S201 water-flow sensor
- 1 × Mini water pump
- 1 × Ultrasonic humidifier
- 1 × Ventilation fan

## Relay Modules

- 3 × Active-low single-channel relay modules

## Audio and Storage

- 1 × DFPlayer Mini
- 1 × Speaker
- 1 × MicroSD card

## Alarm and Warning Components

- 2 × Buzzers
  - 1 × Security buzzer
  - 1 × Fire/blockage alarm buzzer
- 1 × Warning LED
- 1 × LED current-limiting resistor

## Prototyping Components

- 2 × Breadboards
- 1 set × Male-to-male jumper wires
- 1 set × Male-to-female jumper wires
- 1 set × Female-to-female jumper wires

## Power Connections

- 1 × Male DC barrel plug
- 1 × Female DC barrel jack
- 1 × 24 V DC adapter
- 1 × USB cable for Arduino programming and power

## Water System

- 6 mm flexible water pipe
- Pipe from the water tank to the pump
- Pipe from the pump to the flow sensor
- Pipe from the flow sensor to the humidifier or water outlet
- 1 × Water tank/container

## Electrical Connecting Wires

- Red wire for positive supply
- Black wire for ground
- Signal/control wires
